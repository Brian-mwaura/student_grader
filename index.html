Brim

Brim is a form-fresh fast casual restaurant.

At Brim, we use organic ingridients as much as possible & the menu is always gluten free.
We believe in using only extra virgin oil, virgin coconut oil, and avocado oil in our menu items. Our beef is grass fed from Grass Run Farms, our eggs and our chicken is organic and hormone free from Larry Schultz farms in Owatonna , our produce is sourced locally and organic during peak seasons and organically grown in the colder months. We believe the best food is real food made with ingredients rich in nutrition.
